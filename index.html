FOOD SECURITY IS NOT GUARANTEED

Food deserts plague many neighborhoods and housing blocks across the United States. Supermarkets offering fresh food options move out of low-income neighborhoods into high-income ones. The food gap left by their repositioning is filled by fast food options, which seek to offer nutritionally poor, low-quality, food options. Naturally, members of these neighborhoods who have little time and money to buy ingredients and cook, are incentivized to purchase these unhealthy options, perpetuating chronic health conditions and a toxic food culture within these areas.

Our Objective

The goal of this site is to provide healthy alternatives to fast food with interesting and international recipes, while also incorporating lessons on basic cooking and baking skills into these recipes. This site will also help to spread awareness on food issues around the world and basic nutrition. We will always have a few dontation links to organizations who are helping spread food security and equality across the world.
